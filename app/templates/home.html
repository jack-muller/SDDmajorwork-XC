Welcome to the GPS Cross Country Competition

About This Website

This website is your go-to platform for tracking and managing results from the GPS Cross Country (XC) competition. Whether you're an athlete, coach, or spectator, our tools allow you to stay updated with individual and school standings throughout the season. View race results, track performance over time, and see how your school ranks against others.

A Brief History of GPS XC

The GPS (Great Public Schools) Cross Country competition is a storied tradition, bringing together the finest young athletes from some of the most prestigious schools in the region. Established over a century ago, the competition has evolved from humble beginnings into a highly anticipated event on the school sports calendar. Each year, athletes push their limits, not just for personal glory but also for the pride of their schools.
